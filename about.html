About Me

My name is Tey Yun Nee, its obvious that my surname is "Tey", and everyone else will just call me Yun Nee, or some weird nicknames that they made up with. I am an-almost-nineteen-year-old girl that loves to challenge myself alot. I can't really tell if I am more to an extrovert or an introvert because I would love to have some social or interaction with a group of friends, but at the same time it would be great to get myself some "Me-Time" as well. I enjoy both moments and situations as that's all parts of life. I always think that there's no definite distinct to only stay with one kind of personality or lifestyle, because it's not about choosing one of those, instead finding the balance between them would be the way it should really be.

In my opinion, it is a place that is far to travel to literally anywhere. I've been living in Rawang as long as I can remember, eventhough it is not as packed and bustling as Kuala Lumpur, but it gaves me different kidn of childhood and memories in this warm-hearted surrounding.
